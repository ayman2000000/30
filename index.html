Shagle FAQ

How does Shagle work?

Shagle connects you with random people worldwide for video chat. Simply select your gender and who you want to talk to, and you can start chatting with strangers face-to-face.

Is Shagle free?

Yes, Shagle offers free random video chats. However, if you want extra features like gender filters and location filters, there's a premium membership option.

Can I use Shagle safely?

While Shagle takes steps to create a safer chatting environment, remember you're talking to strangers. Keep your personal info private and report any inappropriate behavior.

Is there a Shagle app for mobile?

Shagle can be accessed on mobile devices through a browser, ensuring you can chat with strangers anywhere, but there's no dedicated app for iOS or Android.

Are there age restrictions on Shagle?

Yes, you need to be at least 18 years old and have reached the age of majority where you live to use Shagle, ensuring all users are adults.

Can I choose to chat with people from specific countries?

Premium members can use location filters to connect with people from specific countries, making your chat experience more targeted.

Can I select the gender of the people I chat with?

Yes, Shagle offers gender filters for premium members, allowing you to choose to chat with males, females, or couples.

Can I chat in languages other than English on Shagle?

Shagle is an international platform, so you can encounter and chat with people who speak various languages. The site supports diverse language interactions and you can set your translation preferences in settings.

How long can I chat with someone on Shagle?

Your chat can last as long as both you and the other person want. If one of you wants to end the chat or skip to someone else, you can do so anytime by pressing next.

What if I encounter someone breaking the rules?

Shagle has a reporting system that lets you report anyone who doesn't follow the site's Terms of Use. You can also block users to prevent further contact.
